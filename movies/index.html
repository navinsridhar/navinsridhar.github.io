Movies

Movies and animations from my research simulations.

A small gallery of movies and animations from my research — particle-in-cell simulations of relativistic plasmas, magnetic reconnection in black-hole coronae, and outflows from compact-object engines. Click any video to expand; captions briefly describe the underlying physics and the corresponding publication.

1 · Relativistic magnetic reconnection in pair plasmas

Plasmoid-mediated magnetic reconnection in a relativistic pair plasma. Particles are accelerated to high Lorentz factors at the X-points and advected into the chain of plasmoids that move along the current sheet. From Sridhar+ 2021 (MNRAS 507, 5625).

2 · Highly dynamic black hole jet sheath (candidate site of corona)

Particle-in-cell simulation of relativistic reconnection in an electron–ion plasma at conditions appropriate for the magnetized sheath of a black-hole jet. The animation tracks the dissipation of magnetic energy and the development of the non-thermal particle spectrum that Comptonizes the soft seed photons. From Sridhar+ 2025 (ApJ 979, 199).

3 · Strong electromagnetic waves in unmagnetized pair plasma

A large-amplitude electromagnetic wave — analogous to a fast radio burst pulse — sweeping through an unmagnetized pair plasma in the relativistic regime. The wave bulk-accelerates and heats the plasma, and is partially transmitted while exciting longitudinal modes. From Sridhar, Sobacchi, Sironi+ 2026.

4 · Hypernebula around a hyper-accreting FRB engine

Schematic time evolution of the hypernebula inflated by the wind of a hyper-accreting compact object — the proposed engine of repeating fast radio bursts and their associated persistent radio sources. From Sridhar & Metzger 2022 (ApJ 937, 5).
