A small gallery of movies and animations from my research — particle-in-cell
simulations of relativistic plasmas, magnetic reconnection in black-hole
coronae, and outflows from compact-object engines. Click any video to expand;
captions briefly describe the underlying physics and the corresponding
publication.

---

## 2 · Reconnection in electron–ion plasmas (jet-sheath corona)

<div class="row align-items-center mb-4">
  <div class="col-md-7 mb-3 mb-md-0">
    {% include video.liquid path="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cMmt8FYJI3o" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" width="100%" height="360" title="Reconnection in electron-ion plasma" %}
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-5">
    Particle-in-cell simulation of relativistic reconnection in an
    electron–ion plasma at conditions appropriate for the magnetized sheath
    of a black-hole jet. The animation tracks the dissipation of magnetic
    energy and the development of the non-thermal particle spectrum that
    Comptonizes the soft seed photons. From
    <a href="https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025ApJ...979..199S">Sridhar+ 2025 (ApJ 979, 199)</a>.
  </div>
</div>

## 3 · Strong electromagnetic waves in unmagnetized pair plasma

<div class="row align-items-center mb-4">
  <div class="col-md-7 mb-3 mb-md-0">
    {% include video.liquid path="https://www.youtube.com/embed/REPLACE_VIDEO_ID_3" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1" width="100%" height="360" title="Strong EM wave in unmagnetized pair plasma" %}
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-5">
    A large-amplitude electromagnetic wave — analogous to a fast radio burst
    pulse — sweeping through an unmagnetized pair plasma in the relativistic
    regime. The wave bulk-accelerates and heats the plasma, and is partially
    transmitted while exciting longitudinal modes. From
    <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.11698">Sridhar, Sobacchi, Sironi+ 2026</a>.
  </div>
</div>
